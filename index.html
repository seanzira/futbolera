The Best Footballer To Ever Grace The Pitch

Zinedine Yazid Zinedane, also known by fans as Zizou, is one of the footballers who I believe to be the greatest to ever grace the pitch. With a prolific career starting at Cannes and ending at Real Madrid. He not only stood out because of his exceptional football talent but also because of his accolades and achievements with Real Madrid. As one of the players in the Galacticos era, the accolades he obtained include:

  • 4x Champions League Titles
  • 3x La Liga Titles
  • 4x UEFA Supercup Titles
  • 1x World Cup (1998)
  • 1x UEFA Euro (2000)
  • 1x Ballon d'Or (1998)

"Real Madrid is the most important thing that happened to me, both as a footballer and as a person"
